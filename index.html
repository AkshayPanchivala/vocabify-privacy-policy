Vocabify Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2025

Vocabify is committed to protecting your privacy. This document outlines the information we access and how it's handled within our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

Vocabify does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII). When a user double-clicks a word on a web page, Vocabify reads the selected word and the surrounding text context to fetch a definition. This data is not saved or transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

The selected word is sent to a trusted third-party dictionary API solely for the purpose of displaying the word’s definition, synonyms, and pronunciation in a popup. No information is stored or used for tracking.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Vocabify may rely on third-party dictionary APIs to retrieve word definitions. These services may have their own privacy policies, which are not governed by Vocabify.

5. Data Security

As Vocabify does not store any user data, the risk of data exposure is minimal. All processes are run locally in the user’s browser session.

6. Your Consent

By using Vocabify, you consent to this privacy policy.
