Vocabify Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 17, 2025

Vocabify respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Collect

Vocabify does not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII). To provide definitions, the extension may access the text of a page and the word you double-click. This data is used only to fetch a definition and is not stored.

2. How We Use Information

We use external dictionary APIs to provide definitions. We do not track or store user behavior or content.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share any user data with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use dictionary APIs that have their own privacy policies.
